Hard Constraints

- NO automated flows, NO logging, NO ticketing, and NO runbooks.
- Manual-only refresh for SharePoint content.
- Desktop-first layout: Fixed 16:9 at 1080p resolution.
- Scripts are display-only (copy-ready, never executable).
- App defaults to read-only. Edit Mode requires explicit toggle.
- App must support three Dataverse tables:

  1. OpsKnowledgeHQ
  2. OpsOncallExports
  3. OpsCommands
