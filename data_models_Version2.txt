Data Models

OpsKnowledgeHQ
Purpose: Unified knowledge store for sites, phones, nodes, troubleshooting, references, and operator notes.

Fields:
- Common Fields: Title, RecordType (choice: Site, Phone, Node, etc.), Tags, Summary, Details, Links, OwnerTeam, IsPinned, IsActive, LastVerified, UpdatedByDisplay.
- Site-Specific: SiteCode, SiteName, Address, City, State, Zip, CircuitId, SiteAffiliate, SiteNotes.
- Phone-Specific: ContactName, Department, Role, IvantiPhonePrimary, IvantiPhoneSecondary, StaffDirectoryPhone, OtherContactLabel, OtherContactPhone, Email, AfterHoursNotes.
- Node-Specific: AppGroup, NodeHostname, NodeIPAddress, NodeRole, NodeEnvironment, NodeOwnerTeam.

---

OpsOncallExports
Purpose: Track on-call schedules and roles for triage.

Fields:
- OnCallRole, PrimaryName, PrimaryPhone, SecondaryName, SecondaryPhone.
- CoverageNotes, SharedNotes, Tags, IsActive, LastVerified, UpdatedByDisplay.

Constraints:
Records are read-only with manual refresh to ensure updated schedules.

---

OpsCommands
Purpose: Repository for categorized CMD/PowerShell scripts for manual operator use.

Fields:
- ScriptName, ScriptText, ShellType (CMD or PowerShell), Category, Tags, Summary, ModifiedDate, UpdatedByDisplay.

Constraints:
Scripts are copy-to-clipboard and display-only (not executable). Categorized for quick operator access.
