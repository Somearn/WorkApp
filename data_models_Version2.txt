OpsKnowledgeHQ
Purpose:
A centralized knowledge store for sites, phones, nodes, troubleshooting procedures, references, and operator notes.

Fields:

Common Fields:
Title, RecordType (choice: Site, Phone, Node, etc.), Tags, Summary, Details, Links, OwnerTeam, IsPinned, IsActive, LastVerified, UpdatedByDisplay.
Site-Specific:
SiteCode, SiteName, Address, City, State, Zip, CircuitId, SiteAffiliate, SiteNotes.
Phone-Specific:
ContactName, Department, Role, IvantiPhonePrimary, IvantiPhoneSecondary, StaffDirectoryPhone, OtherContactLabel, OtherContactPhone, Email, AfterHoursNotes.
Node-Specific:
AppGroup, NodeHostname, NodeIPAddress, NodeRole, NodeEnvironment, NodeOwnerTeam.
OpsOncallExports
Purpose:
Tracks on-call schedules and roles for triage operations with functionality to replace all on-call information via Excel import.

Fields:

OnCallRole, PrimaryName, PrimaryPhone, SecondaryName, SecondaryPhone.
CoverageNotes, SharedNotes, Tags, IsActive, LastVerified, UpdatedByDisplay.
Constraints:

Records are read-only with manual refresh to ensure up-to-date schedules.
Includes an Update Button:
Function: Replaces all on-call information via Excel import.
Import Excel Fields:
Primary, Primary Phone, Backup, Backup Phone, Manager, Director, VP, StartDate, EndDate.
Features:

Ensures administrators can update schedules in bulk, avoiding manual entry.
OpsCommands
Purpose:
A repository for categorized CMD/PowerShell scripts for manual operator usage.

Fields:

ScriptName, ScriptText, ShellType (CMD or PowerShell), Category, Tags, Summary, ModifiedDate, UpdatedByDisplay.
Constraints:

Scripts are copy-to-clipboard and display-only (not executable).
Categorized for structured access by operators.
Features:

Built-in copy functionality for rapid sharing and execution outside the app.
